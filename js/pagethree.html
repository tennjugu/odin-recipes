Cabbage sauce

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • pepper
  • onions
  • carrots
  • Vegetable oil
  • tomato paste
  • meat stock / fish
  • some seasonings like, curry powder, dried thyme, seasoning cubes and salt
  • seasoning cubes

Instructions

  1. Put small quantity of groundnut oil in a cooking pot and add chopped onions, sliced tomato balls/paste and chopped pepper to fry.
  2. Add meat stock, shredded beef/chicken or fish and allow to boil.
  3. Add seasoning, salt, curry powder and other spices to taste and allow to boil.
  4. Add carrots, green peppers, red pepper and allow to cook under low heat for 3 minutes.
  5. Add cabbage and allow to steam for 2 minutes.
  6. Mix well and serve with white rice, spaghetti or macaroni.
